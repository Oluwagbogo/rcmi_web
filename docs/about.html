About RCMI & IDC

Understanding our mission, values, and commitment to advancing health disparities research

What is RCMI?

The Research Centers in Minority Institutions (RCMI) program develops and strengthens the infrastructure necessary to conduct state-of-the-art biomedical research and foster the next generation of researchers from underrepresented populations.

The RCMI program provides grants to institutions that award doctoral degrees in the health professions or health-related sciences and have a historical and current commitment to serving students from underrepresented populations. The RCMI program supports NIMHD's vision to advance the science of minority health and health disparities research by enabling all investigators within the program the opportunity to engage in rigorous, mentored research experiences focused on diseases that disproportionately affect minority and other health disparity populations.

Center for Urban Health Disparities Research and Innovation

Morgan State University's RCMI program is housed within the Center for Urban Health Disparities Research and Innovation, which serves as a hub for cutting-edge research addressing health inequities in urban communities.

The Investigator Development Core (IDC)

The Investigator Development Core supports the development and enhances the competitiveness of diverse investigators in basic biomedical, behavioral and/or clinical sciences, with particular emphasis on early-stage investigators.

Core Activities

  • Administration of a competitive pilot grant program for innovative research
  • Provision of comprehensive writing and research support services
  • Community building through seminars, workshops, and networking events
  • Mentorship programs connecting early-stage and established investigators
  • Grant writing workshops and manuscript development assistance
  • Research methodology and statistical analysis support

Our Mission

🎓

Develop Investigators

Nurture the growth of diverse researchers through targeted training, mentorship, and resources that build research capacity and competitiveness.

🔬

Advance Research

Support innovative research that addresses health disparities and contributes to the biomedical knowledge base through pilot grants and infrastructure.

🌟

Foster Excellence

Create an environment where investigators can achieve their full potential through collaboration, support, and access to cutting-edge resources.

Strategic Goals

The IDC operates with a clear vision to transform health disparities research by empowering a diverse community of investigators. Our strategic goals include:

  • Increasing the number of early-stage investigators successfully competing for external funding
  • Building a robust pipeline of diverse researchers in biomedical and behavioral sciences
  • Fostering interdisciplinary collaborations that address complex health challenges
  • Enhancing research infrastructure and support services for all investigators
  • Promoting publication of high-impact research in peer-reviewed journals
  • Creating sustainable pathways for career advancement in health disparities research

Impact & Vision

The RCMI Investigator Development Core at Morgan State University is more than a program—it's a commitment to transforming the landscape of biomedical research by ensuring that diverse voices and perspectives are represented in the scientific community.

Through our comprehensive approach to investigator development, we are building a sustainable ecosystem that supports researchers from their earliest career stages through established independence. Our vision is to create a future where health disparities are eliminated through rigorous scientific inquiry led by a diverse community of investigators who reflect the populations they serve.
