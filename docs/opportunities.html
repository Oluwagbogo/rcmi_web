Application Timeline

Pilot Grant Cycle

Call for Applications

September - October

Annual call for pilot grant proposals distributed to MSU faculty. Information sessions held to answer questions and provide guidance on application process.

Application Deadline

November 1

Complete applications due. All proposals must include research plan, budget, biosketch, and letter of support from department chair.

Review Process

November - December

Expert panel reviews applications based on scientific merit, innovation, potential impact, and investigator qualifications. Top proposals may be invited for interviews.

Award Notification

January

Successful applicants notified and award process initiated. Awardees work with program staff to finalize budgets and establish project timelines.

Project Start

February - March

Funded projects begin. Investigators receive orientation on reporting requirements, core facility access, and support services available through the program.
